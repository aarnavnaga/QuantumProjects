Start the hub first: python3 launcher.py — then click Launch (opens a separate Pygame window).

Foundations

Wavefunction

Schrödinger Evolution

Numerically evolve a quantum wavepacket in real time — visualize Re[ψ], Im[ψ], and |ψ|². Explore tunneling, dispersion, reflection, and interference across five potential landscapes.

Spin-1/2

Bloch Sphere & Qubit Gates

Visualize single-qubit states on an interactive 3D Bloch sphere. Apply X, Y, Z, H, S, T gates as rotations, watch precession, and see measurement probabilities update live.

Deck analogy

Superposition & Entanglement (Playing Cards)

Two modes: a single "quantum card" in superposition until you measure — then a Bell-style pair where Alice and Bob's sealed cards always match ♥♥ or ♠♠, illustrating entanglement.

Advanced Topics

Optimization

QA vs. Gradient Descent Showdown

Watch simulated quantum annealing tunnel through energy barriers that permanently trap stochastic gradient descent — side-by-side on the same loss landscape.

Topological Qubits

Kitaev Chain & Majorana Zero Modes

Explore a toy model of Microsoft's topological qubit — drag sliders across the phase boundary and watch Majorana zero modes emerge at the chain edges.
